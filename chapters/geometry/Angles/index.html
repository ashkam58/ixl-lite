Angle Vocabulary

Definitions

  • Angle: Figure formed by two rays with a common endpoint (the vertex).
  • Adjacent angles: Share a vertex and a side, interiors do not overlap.
  • Vertical angles: Non-adjacent angles formed by two intersecting lines; they are congruent.
  • Complementary: Two angles whose measures add to 90Â°.
  • Supplementary: Two angles whose measures add to 180Â°.
  • Congruent angles: Angles with equal measure.
  • Angle bisector: A ray that splits an angle into two congruent angles.
